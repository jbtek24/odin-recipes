Meat Lover's Omelette Recipe

Description

Here's a simple guide to help you with preparing a meat lover's omelette.

Ingredients

  • 4 eggs
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 3 slices of bacon
  • 1/4 cup of sausage
  • 1/4 cup of ham

Steps

  1. In small bowl, combine eggs & pepper
  2. In a skillet melt the butter
  3. Add egg mixture
  4. As eggs cook, gently lift edges & titlt pan so uncooked portion flows underneath
  5. Cook for 3-4mins
  6. Sprinkle cheese, bacon, sausave, & ham evently onto half of omlelette
  7. Fold omelette in half
  8. Cook for 1min, until cheese is melted
  9. Breakfast is now served
