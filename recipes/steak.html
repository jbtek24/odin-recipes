Ribeye Steak Recipe

Description

Here's a simple guide to help you with preparing a ribeye steak dinner.

Ingredients

  • Rib eye steak
  • Butter
  • Olive oil
  • Fresh herbs
  • Garlic
  • Salt & black pepper

Steps

  1. Bring the steaks to room temp
  2. Season the steaks & make garlic herb butter
  3. Cook the steaks in a skillet on stove 4-5mins on each side
  4. Rest & serve
